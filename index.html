PinPals is a golf-matching app that helps you find golfers nearby who match your skill level, schedule, and style of play.

What is PinPals?

PinPals is a Tinder-style golf matching app designed to help golfers connect with playing partners who share their passion for the game.

Users create a profile with their handicap, skill level, preferred tee times, and home courses.

They swipe through nearby golfers and match with people who want to play at the same level and schedule.

How It Works

person_add

Set up your profile

Add your handicap, skill level (beginner, intermediate, advanced, competitive), and what you're looking for (9 holes, 18 holes, driving range, casual or competitive).

swipe

Match with nearby golfers

See golfers near you based on location and preferences, and swipe to match with people who fit your vibe.

calendar_month

Plan your round

Choose from nearby courses, pick 9 or 18 holes, suggest a time, and send an initial message like: "Want to play 18 at [Course Name] at [Time]?"
